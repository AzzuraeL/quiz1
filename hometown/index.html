Trenggalek

Trenggalek Regency (Javanese: Hanacaraka: ꦠꦽꦁꦒꦭꦺꦏ꧀, Pegon: ترۤڠؼالَيك, translit. Trênggalèk; Javanese pronunciation: [t̪rəŋgaˈlɛk̚]) is a regency in East Java Province, Indonesia. Its capital is Trenggalek District, located about 180 km from Surabaya, the capital city of East Java Province. According to data from the Central Statistics Agency (BPS) in 2023, the regency covers an area of 1,261.40 km² and had a population of 753,810 in 2024. It lies on the southern coast and shares borders with Ponorogo Regency to the north, Tulungagung Regency to the east, the Indian Ocean to the south, and both Pacitan Regency and Ponorogo Regency to the west.
